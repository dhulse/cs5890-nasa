Near Earth Objects

Near earth objects or NEOs are entities that exist in space that orbit near planet Earth. These objects have a variety of sizes, shapes, elements and speeds. A study of NEOs can help us understand the creation of the solar system and where it may be headed in the future. Additionally, we can monitor which asteroids may be potentially hazardous. Finally, element extraction from space objects is a growing field of interest and determining which objects are good candidates for mining is an essential problem to solve.
